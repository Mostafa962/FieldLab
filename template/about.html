How We Started

FieldLab is subsidiary company for our mother company Zone Technologies located at Cairo-Egypt and our sister Prime Zone Systems located at Casablanca Morocco specialized in supplying Medical and Lab solutions to the Egyptian Market. Zone Technologies was founded in 2005, as Egyptian Survey Ltd by Amr Mosa as a sister company for “Zone Survey-1997” Egypt with few customers in the Egyptian Market, they bought an office space in New Maadi- Cairo .the young company managed to make their mark in the business by providing exceptional Technical Support and Technical Training and also by repairing equipment faster than anyone. We are now the largest distributorof positioning systems in Egypt over 28 employees.

We Are Trusted Company

Prime Zone Systems was founded in 2014, as Moroccan Survey Equipment Supplier SARL., as a subsidiary company for “Zone Technologies “Egypt with no customers in the Moroccan market, After 7 years Prime Zone Systems are now largest distributor or positioning systems in Morocco over 20 employees and many distribution agreements with multinationals manufactures from Switzerland, Germany ,UK and US. For over 16 years Zone Technologies has helped clients capture, transform and manage data leading to increase efficiency and probability.
